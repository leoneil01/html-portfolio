The Best Movies According to Leoneil

My top 3 movies of all-time

Avatar: The Way of Water

This is the continuation of the first film, Avatar. It is Sci-Fi movie co-produced and directed by Jame Cameron.

Spider-Man: No Way Home

This is another great film produced by Marvel Studios. It is a superhero film based on the Marvel Comics character Spider-Man. The interesting part here is, there are 3 spider-man in the movie. How cool is that?

One Piece Film: Red

This is an anime movie. It’s an action-adventure film produced by Toei Animation and is based on a manga written and illustrated by Eiichiro Oda. In this film, for the first time, Luffy’s Gear 5 powers are showcased.
